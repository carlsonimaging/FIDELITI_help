About FIDELITI
The FIDELITI Dashboard provides visualizations of individualized neuroimaging biomarkers at a glance in an easy-to-use dashboard.
Installation
The FIDELITI Dashboard is currently available for MacOS. Click for download and installation instructions.
Participants
Each participant can have one imaging session or multiple sessions, allowing for quantification of potential changes over time.
Processing
FIDELITI has built-in validated neuroimaging processing pipelines to automatically process your participants' imaging.
Dashboard
The dashboard page is the heart of FIDELITI, displaying multiple graphs with normative modelling results for your participant.
Demonstration
A demonstration page is provided for exploring functionality for those users who have not yet processed their own data.
Profiles
Dashboard content can be customized depending on your participant and project-specific needs.
Preferences
The Dashboard can be customized via preferences set by you according to your needs.
